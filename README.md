# Chrome-Personal-Startup
You can set Chrome to open a New Tab page whenever you open a new window.

On your computer, open Chrome Chrome.
At the top right, click More More and then Settings.
On the left, click On startup. 
Click Open the New Tab page.

You can customize your New Tab page with:

Your shortcuts 
Recipes you viewed
Your shopping carts on other sites
Different themes 

I have made my own chrome you can download it and change it according to your choice .
You just need the basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript
